## Output Template: Jira Epic

Generate a Jira epic using the following structure. Audience is the technical team. Do not add sections beyond what is listed.

**Business Context**
Two to four sentences explaining why this work is needed. Include the business problem or opportunity being addressed and the expected outcome. Do not describe implementation.

**Impacted Systems**
Bulleted list of systems, services, or components affected by this epic. Draw from the meeting context. No attribution to individuals.

**Scope**
Bulleted list of what is included in this epic. Each item is a goal-oriented outcome, not a task. Focus on what will be true when the epic is complete.

**Acceptance Criteria**
Numbered list of high-level, observable outcomes that define done at the epic level. Goal-oriented. No implementation steps or technical instructions.

**Technical Notes**
Bulleted list of relevant technical context, constraints, decisions, or background from the meeting. No direct attribution to individuals. Present as factual observations.
