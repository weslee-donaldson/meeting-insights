## Output Template: Thread Discovery

Analyze the meeting context and identify trackable issues, decisions, or ongoing topics that would benefit from cross-meeting tracking. For each identified thread, provide:

**Thread Title**
A concise, descriptive name for the issue or topic being tracked.

**Shorthand**
A 2-6 character abbreviation (e.g., "AUTH", "PERF", "MIGR").

**Description**
Two to four sentences describing the issue, decision, or topic. Include the current state and why it warrants tracking across meetings.

**Criteria Prompt**
A short instruction describing what makes a future meeting relevant to this thread. Example: "Mentions authentication flow, SSO integration, or login issues."

**Related Context**
Bulleted list of specific evidence from the meeting — decisions made, blockers raised, owners identified. No attribution to individuals.

If multiple trackable threads are identified, separate them with a horizontal rule (---).
